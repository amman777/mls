Wardrobe FAQ's

What is a modular wardrobe?
A customizable storage unit with various finishes, layouts, and accessories.
What materials are used for modular wardrobes?
Plywood, MDF, particle board, HDHMR, veneer, glass, and acrylic.
What are the different door styles available?
Hinged, sliding, and walk-in wardrobes.
Can I customize the wardrobe to fit my needs?
Yes, customization includes internal layout, finishes, and storage options.
How do I maintain a modular wardrobe?
Clean with a soft damp cloth and avoid harsh chemicals.
