Kitchen FAQ's

What is a modular kitchen?
A kitchen design concept with pre-manufactured cabinet modules for efficient storage and functionality.
What are the key benefits of a modular kitchen?
Customizability, space optimization, easy maintenance, and stylish designs.
What materials are commonly used in modular kitchens?
Plywood, MDF, HDF, HDHMR, stainless steel, acrylic, quartz, and granite.
How long does it take to install a modular kitchen?
Typically, installation takes about 1-2 weeks.
How do I maintain a modular kitchen?
Regular cleaning with mild soap; avoid abrasive cleaners.
